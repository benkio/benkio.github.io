Homo Deus

Homo Deus - Wikipedia's Page

Super interesting essay from Harari about what could be the future into the XI century. This book makes you think about a lot of topics: intelligence, mind, psychology, technology… It contains a lot of references and bibliography, so you can check out the sources and, because of it, I added a couple of books to my TODO read list. Even if sometimes it seems to overflow into science-fiction realm, it's Super recommended!

The Lord of the Rings

The Lord of the Rings - Wikipedia's page

The Hobbit - Wikipedia's page

I read this one on my teenage years and I ain't gonna lie, it was hard to carry on sometimes. In several parts it's quite slow and descriptive, but it's definitely worth reading. Milestone of fantasy literature.

Shannara

Shannara - Wikipedia's Page

I almost read every book on this series. Even if sometimes it could be very similar to other fantasy operas, like The Lord of the Rings, if you like magic, dragons, elves, dwarfs… you aren't going to be disappointed.
